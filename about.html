OUR BACKGROUND


The organization name is Persatuan Kebajikan Darul Hanan. The name of the organization, which translates to "House of Love," alludes to a safe haven that provides its occupants with illumination in pursuit of a more ideal existence. This group aims to help orphans, the poor, and the less fortunate by providing them with care and compassion from its female members. Those in need, especially orphans, the poor, and the less fortunate, have benefited from the Persatuan Kebajikan Darul Hanan's selfless service and the closer bond of love it has fostered among them. The Association moved forward with plans to build a shelter for abandoned children and those from low-income backgrounds in Pasir Gudang, Johor Bahru. His organization takes its mandate to create a compassionate society from the Persatuan Kebajikan Darul Hanan.

THE FOUNDATION OF THE PERSATUAN KEBAJIKAN DARUL HANAN ESTABLISHMENT
1. Persatuan Kebajikan Darul Hanan was founded in 1992 out of a sense of responsibility on the part of YBhg Puan Sri Datin Noorzilah, the organization's founder and chairman, and her committee members to foster love among the less fortunate and defend the fate of orphaned children.
2. As a result of the members' efforts, the association was able to establish Darul Hanan, a home for orphans and the poor, on an endowed 4-acre plot of land near the Masjid Jamik Pasir Gudang.
3. The goals of Darul Hanan's establishment are to provide service to the community, particularly orphans and poor children who have not yet received care and protection, to place an emphasis on their education so they can attain a higher standard of living.
4. The first building of Darul Hanan was constructed in 1992, and on 10 January 1993, 31 girls enrolled.
5. On November 21, 1995, Darul Hanan began accepting a group of 31 boys; since then, the organisation has aided more students.
6. A total number of Darul Hanan children have successfully completed their studies at the Institute of Higher Education (IPT), and some of them remain under the care of Darul Hanan.
7. Children from Darul Hanan are also encouraged to participate in sports and other extracurricular activities.
8. Pasir Gudang's rapid transition from a fishing community to an industrial zone necessitates a social equilibrium in which the needs of the less fortunate are protected alongside those of the more fortunate.
9. The decision was motivated by the realisation that no one else will assist the numerous orphans and poor children in the area, as well as the desire to make a positive contribution to the local community.


THE CONSTRUCTION CONCEPT OF THE PERSATUAN KEBAJIKAN DARUL HANAN
1. Built on a 4-acre plot of land donated by the State Government in the name of PKENJ with special conditions to be used for the Orphanage Hostel Building Site and other related buildings.
2. As apply family characteristics to avoid social problems later on, the construction was made based on the selection of a special two-storey bungalow concept each for girls and boys.
3. In order to finance the cost of this construction, the Darul Hanan has implemented various projects to fill special funds by holding charity night sales, sales of used goods, donations & contributions from companies.
4. the Darul Hanan Welfare Association has made a step forward by building a Multi-Purpose Hall "Dewan Ajwa" for the convenience of its residents with the proceeds from the Ramadhan Date Charity Sale.


MISSIONS

Contributing service to the community, especially orphans and poor children who have not yet received attention and protection.

Emphasis on learning to achieve a better standard of living.

Applying positive family values to curb social problems in the future.

Support the concept of a caring society and a loving society.

Cooperate with associations, organizations and other bodies that are registered and have the same purpose.


VISIONS



To defend the fate of those who are less fortunate, may they become members of society who can build and defend the nation and family.

Want to see more Darul Hanan children achieve success in the field of education and succeed in furthering their studies at higher education centres.

It is hoped that the children of Darul Hanan will be able to apply positive family values to each other and the closeness of friendship so that they do not break up when they leave Darul Hanan later.

It is Darul Hanan's hope that it can continue to be a place of `shelter’ that gives light to less fortunate children and ensures that the children of Darul Hanan can have a perfect life for their future.

More people are willing to come forward to donate to the Darul Hanan Management Fund to finance the life and learning of the children here and welcome donations in the form of money and material things.
