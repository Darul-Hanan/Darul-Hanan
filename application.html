ADMISSIONS AND APPLICATION PROCESS FOR CHILDREN AT DARUL HANAN

The children who are eligible to live in Darul Hanan are orphans and children from poor families. However, if the child is an orphan, the heir's economic criteria are prioritized. It is best that the child taken is in school and children who are not in school can only be considered according to merit. All admission of children must be approved by the Darul Hanan Management Committee.



THE FOUNDATION OF THE PERSATUAN KEBAJIKAN DARUL HANAN ESTABLISHMENT


1. First Procedure: The applicant must fill in the provided Form completely with the documents
➤ Copy of identification card
➤ Birth certificate
➤ Death certificate
➤ The heir's salary slip
➤ Confirmation from the employer
➤ Types of aid received if any
➤ Candidate's latest exam results and support from the school
2. Second Procedure: Darul Hanan officials will visit the homes of the applicants who have been listed to obtain more information related to the applicant's real life for the purpose of further evaluation.
3. Third Procedure: The results of the visit will be presented to the Darul Hanan Management Committee meeting for acceptance / rejection. Candidates will be notified of the Committee's decision.
